    What is CIBIL Score and why is it important for Personal Loan?
    by Admin
    Category: Business, Finances
    0 Comments

    CIBIL (Credit Information Bureau, India Limited) prepares a credit score which acts as the first criterion to check the eligibility of a person for a personal loan. This credit score is a three-digit numeric summary of your entire credit history. This is based on the information provided by banks and other financial organizations on a monthly basis whose value ranges from 300 to 900. A score lower than 750 is considered as a low CIBIL score.
